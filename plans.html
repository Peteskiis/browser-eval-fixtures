Subscription plans

Three fictional tiers, described in prose on purpose — comparing them takes some reading.

Seedling

Seedling costs $7/mo. It includes two projects, one seat, and daily backups. Its distinguishing feature is the free community workshop every month.

Grove

Grove costs $41/mo. It includes twenty projects, five seats, and hourly backups. Its distinguishing feature is single sign-on support.

Canopy

Canopy costs $113/mo. It includes unlimited projects and seats plus continuous backups. Its distinguishing feature is a dedicated support engineer.
